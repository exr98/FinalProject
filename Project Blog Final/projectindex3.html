China Town Bakery @ Leicester Square

Chinese Bakery


Rating ... 4.5/5

Price ... £

Specialty ... Taiyaki and Chinese buns


Thoughts

Ever since coming to UK, I have been craving for Eastern Asian desserts. Finally I have my chance today. I tried the Icy Taro Ball 4 which costs £8. It was AMAZING!!! The portion was massive, I couldn't even finish the whole bowl. I would say it's worth going to, even though the price is a bit expensive, but you can always share with someone. It's a nice place to hang out for a dessert after a meal in China Town or theatre nearby. But BEWARE there's not a lot of place to sit. SO maybe get a takeaway instead and eat infront of the TV while enjoying a soap opera.
